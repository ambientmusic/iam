This privacy policy applies only to the actions of Information Technology Governance Limited and the User with respect to this Website. It does not extend to any websites that can be accessed from this Website.
For the puposes of the applicable Data Protection Laws, Information Technology Governance Limited is the 'Data Controller' this means that Information Technology Governance Limited determines the purpose for which, and the manner in which, your data is processed.
The Website was developed in a collaboration between Spotify and Information Technology Governance Limited and is subject to the Spotify Developer Terms of Service. The User data processed by the Website is confined to certain item's of Spotify account data. Further, this data is only processed if expressly authorised by the User. It comprises
a) Your Public Playlists - access to this data is technically feasible although it is not processed by the Website.
b) The Track You're Playing - access is made to enable synchronised lyrics translation for enhanced User appreciation of music and User language learning purposes.
c) The Type of Device You're Listening On - access is technically feasible and may be processed by the Website to enhance website presentation to Users.
The User can easily reverse permission to access any and all of the the data specified (at a, b and c above) at anytime by clicking REMOVE ACCESS from 'ambientmusic' in the Apps sub folder of the User's Spotify Account.
Spotify credentials are needed to access this website however they are only visible to Spotify and they are subject to the User's agreement with Spotify (see Spotify's privacy policy).
